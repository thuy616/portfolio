Estia - Spirit of the Building

Concept for 3D City Model Hackathon

Estia group developed a data-driven adaptive LEDs lighting and way finding system that promotes space sharing for energy saving and also allows the building to communicate with people through lighting and multiple devices. We started this Hackathon by thinking about how buildings communicate with people. How can all the data of buildings such as heating, electricity usage, water usage, and occupancy be effectively communicated to the people who are using the building. The whole concept was to avoid buildings where one receives the information through smartphones but to have a system that integrates the information with the actual interior design and the architecture of the building. At the back end there is an analytic model of energy data to visualize such data in a deeper way for people to understand how they use their energy.

The World Economy through Oil Data

Course Project - Exploratory Data Visualization - Aalto University

Oil data is complex because it covers many countries, spans over many years, and has many dimensions. The aim of this visualization is to communicate the complex oil data with clarity and precision to the audience who are not experts in the field. “The World Econ- omy through Oil Data” visualization also explores the storytelling elements to guide audience through an interpretation path to answer following questions: Why are oil prices falling so much now? Who benefits from low oil prices? Who suffers? How oil data relates to the world economy?
